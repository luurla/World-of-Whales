    What is this All about?

    Exploring the fascinating

    world of whales

    Whale Facs started as a passion project during my time in the Ment2 module at university. As an aspiring coder, I wanted to create a platform where I could combine my love for whales with my newfound coding skills. The result? A whimsical and entertaining website that celebrates these majestic creatures.

    While Whale Facs may not be your typical serious and structured resource on whales, it serves a unique purpose. It's a playground where I could experiment with different coding techniques, try out new design ideas, and showcase my growth as a developer.

    One of the aspects that makes Whale Facs truly special is the use of artificial intelligence (AI) in generating the pictures. By leveraging AI algorithms, some of the images on the website take on a weird and funny appearance. This adds an extra layer of whimsy and unexpectedness to the experience. So don't be surprised if you come across a grinning whale or a tail doing a dance!

    Here at Whale Facs, we embrace the chaos and find joy in the unexpected. You'll find a mix of informative content, interactive features, and quirky animations that bring the world of whales to life. Dive into a sea of fascinating facts about various whale species, explore mesmerizing photos and videos, and embark on whimsical adventures through our virtual ocean.

    Welcome to Whale Facs - where coding meets chaos, and whales steal the show, with a touch of AI-induced hilarity!
